Welcome to Me!

My Beginnings

I was born in Fairbury, Nebraska to 2 hippies that were trying to be self substaining farmers. I developed a special bond with horses and up through my teenage years, I rode and trained horses in the boondocks of Nebraska.

I graduated from Norris High School with 126 kids in my class. In 2004, it was wiped out by a tornado and had to be rebuilt.
